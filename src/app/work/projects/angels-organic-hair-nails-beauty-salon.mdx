## Overview

This project is a modern, mobile-first website developed for **Angel's Organic Hair-Nails & Beauty**, a boutique beauty salon located in Melbourne, Australia. The goal was to create a visually appealing, fast-loading, and SEO-friendly platform to showcase the salon’s services, team, pricing, and contact information — all while ensuring a great experience on both desktop and mobile devices.

## Key Features

- **Responsive Design**: Fully responsive layout optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop users using flexible grid and typography systems.
- **SEO Optimization**: Implemented meta tags, structured data, Open Graph tags, and semantic HTML to enhance discoverability on Google and social platforms.
- **Service Showcase**: Dedicated sections for hair, nail, and skincare services, with clear descriptions, images, and pricing.
- **Contact & Booking**: Integrated Google Maps, click-to-call phone number, and contact form for booking inquiries.
- **Gallery Section**: Dynamic gallery to highlight the salon’s organic products and real customer transformations.
- **Performance Focus**: Lightweight frontend with optimized assets, lazy-loading, and accessibility best practices for faster page loads.

## Technologies Used

- **React.js**: Developed the frontend UI using functional components and hooks.
- **HTML & CSS (SCSS)**: Used semantic HTML5 and modular SCSS for maintainable styles.
- **Vercel**: For fast and reliable deployment with preview URLs and global CDN.
- **Cloudflare**: Used for DNS and performance optimization including HTTPS and caching.
- **SEO Tools**: Manual tuning with Lighthouse audits, meta config, and schema.org integration.

## Challenges and Learnings

A key challenge was maintaining visual consistency while supporting a broad range of screen sizes and browsers. Additionally, balancing SEO, load performance, and design quality required fine-tuning assets and structure. Ensuring accessibility and complying with WCAG standards also contributed to the learning curve.

## Outcome

The website has helped **Angel's Organic Hair-Nails & Beauty** increase local visibility, attract new customers through organic search, and reduce booking friction for mobile users. The client has reported increased walk-ins and improved online engagement since the launch. The project is also easy to maintain and update, supporting the salon's growth in Melbourne's competitive beauty market.
